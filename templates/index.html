Information for New Students & Teachers

#}
  • To log in to Utron, you need to have a network ID. This consist of two things: Your username, which is the Utron code you were given at the time of registration in school. Your password, which is a combination of your surname and the last two digits of the year you were born.

    For example: If your surname is 'ogbonna' and your year of birth is '2010', your password will be 'ogbonna10'.
  • If you have forgotten your password, please request a password reset from your school.
  • Making Payments via Utron
    You can make payments like tuition fees on the Utron platform instead of visiting the bank.
